Send Feedback

We'd love to hear what you think about SmartGymTimer! Your feedback helps us make the app better for everyone.

How to Send Feedback

Send your thoughts to: haqmalebhaam2025@gmail.com

What Kind of Feedback Are We Looking For?

What You Like

Tell us what's working well for you. What features do you use most? What makes SmartGymTimer helpful for your workouts?

What Could Be Better

Is there anything that's confusing or doesn't work as expected? What frustrations have you encountered while using the app?

Feature Requests

What new features would you like to see? What would make SmartGymTimer even more useful for your fitness routine?

What to Include in Your Feedback

  • Your experience level: Are you new to working out or an experienced athlete?
  • How you use SmartGymTimer: What type of workouts do you typically do?
  • Specific examples: The more specific you can be, the better we can understand your feedback
  • Your device: What device and browser are you using?

How We Use Your Feedback

When we receive feedback:

  • We read every message carefully
  • We look for patterns and common requests
  • We prioritize changes based on what will help the most users
  • We consider all suggestions for future updates

Our Feedback Promise

We may not be able to respond to every feedback message individually, but we read them all and use them to guide our development decisions.

Common Feature Requests We're Considering

  • Customizable rest times
  • Workout history and tracking
  • Different timer sounds and alerts
  • Mobile app versions
  • Social features and sharing
  • Exercise library and preset workouts

Thank You!

We appreciate you taking the time to share your thoughts. Your feedback is valuable and helps us continue improving SmartGymTimer.
