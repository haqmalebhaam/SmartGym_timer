Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I use the timer?

Just press Start when you begin your workout, then press Set Complete after each set. The timer will track your rest time automatically.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes! SmartGymTimer works on phones, tablets, and computers. It's designed to be responsive and work well on any device.

Do I need to create an account?

No, you can use SmartGymTimer without creating an account. Your data is stored locally in your browser.

Features & Functionality

Can I change the rest time?

Right now it uses a standard rest time, but we're working on adding custom timing options in a future update.

How does the sensor work?

The sensor can automatically detect when you finish a set. For best results, place your device on a stable surface near your workout area.

What do the different stats mean?

  • Sets Completed: How many sets you've done
  • Current Rest: How long you've been resting since your last set
  • Best Set Time: Your shortest rest time between sets
  • Total Rest Time: All your rest time added together

Data & Privacy

Is my data saved?

Your data is stored temporarily in your browser, but it's not saved to our servers. If you clear your browser data, your workout history will be lost.

Do you track my workouts?

No, we don't track your individual workouts. Your data stays on your device.

Can I export my workout data?

Not currently, but we're considering adding export features in future versions.

Troubleshooting

The timer stopped working

Try refreshing the page. If that doesn't work, clear your browser cache and try again.

The sensor isn't detecting my sets

Make sure your device is on a stable surface and there's good lighting. You can also try recalibrating the sensor.

My workout data disappeared

This usually happens if you cleared your browser data. Unfortunately, we can't recover locally stored data once it's cleared.

Still Have Questions?

If you didn't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us and we'll be happy to help!
