Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We respect your privacy. Basically, we don't collect your personal data unless you give it to us directly.

In Simple Terms

We don't sell your information to anyone. We only use data to make SmartGymTimer work better for you.

Information We Collect

Workout Data: When you use SmartGymTimer, we store your workout data (sets, rest times, etc.) locally in your browser. This data stays on your device and isn't sent to our servers.

Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we'll keep your email address and message until we've helped you with your question. After that, we'll delete it unless you give us permission to keep it for updates.

Technical Information: We may collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how people use SmartGymTimer and fix bugs. This doesn't include personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To make SmartGymTimer work properly
  • To respond to your questions and support requests
  • To improve the app based on how people use it
  • To fix bugs and technical issues

Data Sharing

We don't share your personal data with third parties. The only exceptions would be if we're required to by law, or if we need to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Ask what data we have about you
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Opt out of any communications
  • Use SmartGymTimer without creating an account
