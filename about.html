About SmartGymTimer

We're a small team of fitness enthusiasts and developers who got tired of constantly checking our phones during workouts.

We created SmartGymTimer to solve that problem - a simple, smart timer that helps you keep track of your rest periods without any fuss.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help people make the most of their gym time by taking the guesswork out of timing. We believe that better timing leads to better workouts, and better workouts lead to better results.

What Makes Us Different

  • We focus on what really matters - your rest time between sets
  • Our sensor technology helps automate your timing
  • We keep things simple and easy to use
  • No complicated setup or learning required

Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or you're a seasoned athlete, SmartGymTimer is here to help you get the most out of every workout session.

We're constantly working to improve SmartGymTimer based on feedback from users like you. If you have suggestions or ideas, we'd love to hear from you!
