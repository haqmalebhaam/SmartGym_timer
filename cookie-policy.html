Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device. They help websites remember your preferences and settings.

Our Approach

We use cookies to make SmartGymTimer work properly. We don't use cookies to track you across the internet or show you targeted ads.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: These are necessary for SmartGymTimer to function. They remember your settings and workout data.

Preference Cookies: These remember your preferences like dark/light mode or sound settings.

Analytics Cookies: We might use anonymous analytics cookies to understand how people use SmartGymTimer. This helps us improve the app.

What We Don't Do

  • We don't use advertising cookies
  • We don't use tracking cookies to follow you around the web
  • We don't sell cookie data to third parties
  • We don't use cookies for marketing purposes

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies

What Happens If You Disable Cookies

If you disable cookies in your browser, SmartGymTimer might not work as well. Specifically:

  • Your settings might not be saved between sessions
  • Your workout history might not be preserved
  • Some features might not work properly

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, SmartGymTimer uses browser local storage to save your workout data and settings. This is similar to cookies but allows us to store more data.

Like cookies, you can clear local storage through your browser settings.
